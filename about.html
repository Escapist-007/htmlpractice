About

HTML practice was created by someone with too much time on their hands. This project was started in early 2017, dedicated to creating actual projects that people can do to practice their HTML skills. At the time that this was created, there was no site for just projects. This site is trying to fill in that gap, and give people something to do.

Learning Sources

This site was not made to teach people anything. If you do want to learn things, Khan Academy has a course that is an excellent introduction to these webpages. Codecademy also has an great selection of courses for learning, as does Code School. There are quite a few books in existence that will help you learn, so go over to your local library. Don't forget about Google!
